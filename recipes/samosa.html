Samosa

A samosa (/səmoʊsə/) or singara is a fried or baked pastry with a savory filling, including ingredients such as spiced potatoes, onions, and peas. It may take different forms, including triangular, cone, or half-moon shapes, depending on the region.

Ingredients

  • potato
  • onion
  • flour
  • chilli pepper
  • salt
