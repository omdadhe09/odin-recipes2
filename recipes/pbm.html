Paneer Butter Masala

An authentic Indian Cuisine famous around Punjab.

So here's the thing with paneer butter masala. If you are a paneer lover, it is the king of all paneer recipes. I've eaten versions of this across the length and breadth of the country (favourite order obviously) and a lot of people can get it very very wrong.

Ingredients

  • butter
  • paneer
  • onions
  • herbs

Steps To Make This Dish

  1. Grind 2 medium onions,tomatoes and garlic
  2. heat 2 table spoons of cooking oil
  3. Add 2 green chillies
  4. Stir and cook for 2 mins
  5. Add paneer and stir for 2 mins
  6. Your curry is ready
